Exit

You can only leave MTKutility from the Home screen.  You have 2 choices for leaving - Exit in the popout menu or double clicking your phone's back button.  The exit is not immediate as there is a list of activities that are checked and shut down to accomplish a clean shutdown.

NOTE: please disConnect before you exit.  This will ensure that your exit is clean.  Disconnecting is part of the shut down list but it has occasionally failed.

Double clicking your phone's back button will also navigate back to the home screen.  Please use the with care as it will abort any activity that is in progress on the screen you are working with at the moment.
