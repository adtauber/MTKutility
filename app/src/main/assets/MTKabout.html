Welcome to MTKutility.  This, my 2nd rewrite, was driven by problems people were having with the log download.  The development objective remains leaving my laptop at home by replicating the PC app functionality in Android.  I am deeply indebted to Bastiaan Naber's having left source code for AndroidMTK on the internet and Keisuke Suzuki's AndroidUSBSerialMonitorLite and FTdriver.

This started as an AGPS data update to a OTG connected Qstarz GPS logger.  But evolved to Bluetooth when I could not get the AGPS update to work.  Bluetooth is more finicky but I've succeeded in getting the full spectrum of GPS logger maintenance into one app.

The development environment is Android Studio, an ancient Samsung SGHI717 with Android 4.2.1 and an ASUS_X00ID with Android 8.1.  The finished app includes the GPX conversion fragment from Bastiaan Naber's AndroidMTK (Nov 2014 version).
